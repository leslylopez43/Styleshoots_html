Guatemala's markets are a sensory delight, where you can experience the vibrant colors and traditions of indigenous communities. Chichicastenango Market is famous for its textiles, crafts, and unique atmosphere. Antigua's markets offer a blend of local and international goods.

Antigua Guatemala, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a picturesque colonial town with cobblestone streets, colorful buildings, and a wealth of cultural attractions. The city is surrounded by volcanoes and is renowned for its preserved Baroque-influenced architecture.

Coffee is one of the main products of the Guatemalan economy since the 1850s; during the twentieth century and the first decade of the 21st century, Guatemala was the Central American country with the highest coffee production, it is currently the tenth coffee producer worldwide.
